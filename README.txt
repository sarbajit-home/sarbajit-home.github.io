Sarbajit's Homepage
===================

This is the repository for my personal portfolio website, showcasing my academic journey at IISER Kolkata, my research interests in Physics and Computer Science, and my technical notes.

Project Structure
-----------------
* **index.html**: The main landing page featuring "About Me", "Interest", "Expertise", and "Experience" sections.
* **notes.html**: A dedicated page for my study notes (MLP, RNN, etc.) and resources.
* **photogallery.html**: A gallery of personal and stock photos.
* **assets/**: Contains CSS, JavaScript, and font files.
* **images/**: Personal photos and project images.
* **notes/**: PDF resources.
